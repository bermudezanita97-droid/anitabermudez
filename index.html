LitFolio

Learn from what you read.

LitFolio transforms books and long-form documents into guided learning experiences with lessons, key concepts, quizzes, review, and audio-friendly interaction. It is built for people who want actual understanding and retention, not just a quick summary.

Who it is for

Serious readers, autodidacts, students, and knowledge workers who want to absorb and apply complex material.

How it works

Upload a nonfiction book or PDF, generate a course structure, move through bite-sized modules, and reinforce learning with quizzes, prompts, and review.

Why it is different

Most tools summarize. LitFolio teaches. The product is designed around comprehension, retention, and usable knowledge.

01 · Upload

Bring in a nonfiction PDF or long-form document worth actually learning from.

02 · Structure

Turn it into a clear curriculum with modules, concepts, lessons, and learning objectives.

03 · Learn

Move through bite-sized units with quizzes, prompts, and audio-friendly learning on the go.

04 · Retain

Revisit the material through review and progress tracking so it becomes usable knowledge.
